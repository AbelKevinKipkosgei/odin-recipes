Recipe description

Egg fried rice is a simple yet flavorful dish that combines fluffy rice with scrambled eggs, aromatics, and seasonings. Itâ€™s a staple in many Asian cuisines because of its versatility and ease of preparation. By stir-frying cooked rice with whisked eggs, soy sauce, and vegetables, the dish develops a rich, savory taste with just a few ingredients. The balance of textures-soft rice, tender eggs, and crisp vegetables-makes it both satisfying and comforting.

This recipe is also highly adaptable, allowing you to incorporate leftover rice and customize with ingredients like peas, carrots, spring onions, or even bits of chicken or shrimp. Its quick cooking time and minimal preparation make egg fried rice an excellent choice for busy weekdays or as a side dish to complement other main courses. With its combination of simplicity, nutrition, and taste, egg fried rice has become a beloved favorite worldwide.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup uncooked instant rice
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ cup sliced green beans or peas
  • 1 large egg, lightly beaten
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
