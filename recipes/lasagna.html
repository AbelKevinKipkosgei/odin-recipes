Recipe description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with a rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta mixture, and plenty of cheese. The meat sauce is usually prepared with ground beef or pork simmered in tomatoes, onions, garlic, and herbs like basil and oregano to develop deep, savory flavors. Between the layers of pasta and sauce, ricotta or béchamel adds creaminess, while mozzarella provides the gooey, stretchy texture that makes lasagna so comforting. Parmesan is often sprinkled on top for extra sharpness and to create a golden-brown crust during baking.

The dish is assembled in a baking dish by alternating layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese until it reaches the top, finishing with a generous layer of cheese. Once baked, the lasagna rests to allow the layers to set, making it easier to cut into neat squares. This hearty, flavorful dish is loved worldwide not just for its taste but also for its versatility, as it can be adapted with vegetables, seafood, or even fully vegetarian versions. Lasagna is often prepared for gatherings and family meals because it’s both filling and satisfying, making it the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (800 grams) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (180 grams) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (170 grams) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1½ teaspoons salt, divided, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon fennel seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 340 grams ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 340 grams mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

Step 1

Gather all your ingredients.

Step 2

Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.

Step 3

Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1½ hours, stirring occasionally.

Step 4

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.

Step 5

In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and ½ teaspoon salt.

Step 6

Preheat the oven to 190 degrees celsius.

Step 7

To assemble, spread 1½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with ½ of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with ⅓ of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with ¼ cup Parmesan cheese.

Step 8

Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.

Step 9

Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.

Step 10

Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
