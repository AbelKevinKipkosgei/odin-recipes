Recipe description

Crispy fried chicken is a timeless comfort food loved for its crunchy golden coating and juicy, tender meat inside. The secret to achieving the perfect crisp lies in seasoning the chicken well and coating it in a flavorful flour mixture before frying. When fried at the right temperature, the exterior becomes beautifully crunchy while sealing in the natural juices of the chicken.

This dish is versatile and pairs wonderfully with a variety of sides such as mashed potatoes, coleslaw, fries, or even fresh salad. Whether served at family gatherings, picnics, or casual dinners, crispy fried chicken never fails to bring people together.
